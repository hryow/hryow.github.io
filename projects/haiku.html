make-your-own haiku

web development

toolkit

Languages Used: HTML/CSS, Javascript, Node.js

the project

+ github

Based on playhtml.fun, this is a website where you can make a haiku out of "fridge magnets." Each line of dots follows the general structure of a haiku, and the arrows at the bottom allow you to navigate to different sets of words. The magnets themselves are randomly placed around the site, and the user drags them to the dots to fill out their custom haiku!
