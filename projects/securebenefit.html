+ website

At Secure Benefit, I was tasked with developing their company's website, which included pages such as their services, the different kinds of employee benefits they offer, and the carrier partners they have. I was also in charge of designing the overall look of the site, and making sure that it was in line with their already existing logo.

The biggest challenge that I faced with this site development was probably the sheer amount of pages. I had never done a website with so many pages (18, to be exact!), and I felt a little overwhelmed with how many files I ended up making, as each page needed its own customized look that aligned with the site's overall design.
