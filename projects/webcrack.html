the project

+ github (website) + github (extension) + devpost

WebCrack is a Chrome extension that is designed to scan online articles for potential bias through analysis of the source and author. Added functionality includes estimating read time and suggesting additional articles with differing perspectives. The goal of this project, submitted for the 2024 Hack for Humanity Hackathon, was to empower ESL (English as a Second Language) individuals by simplifying the consumption of online information, and making it easier for these people to recognize potential biases and misinformation.
