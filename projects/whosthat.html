4-panel comic

the project

This is a 4-panel comic about an encounter with a strange large, dark figure. I first started with rough sketches, planning out where the word bubbles would go. However, I received feedback that the storyline was hard to follow and understand. Following this feedback, I revised the storyline to make it easier to read and understand.

Another criticism that I got from the rough sketches was that the figure wasn't menacing enough to warrant the amount of surprise that my character showed. Therefore, since I liked the animation on my character's face, I revised the figure to look more menacing by making it visibly larger than my characters and modeling it after the physique of bodybuilders.
